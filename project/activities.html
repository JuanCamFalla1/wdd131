San Miguel Party

The San Miguel festival is a vibrant celebration of the city's rich culture. It's a time for comparsas, dances, and beautifully adorned cars as revelers dance through the night in honor of their patron saint.

Tiquipaya Flower Fair

As the name of the city suggests, thanks to its standardized climate throughout the year, the city has ample opportunity for plants to be grown year-round, and in season, their beauty adorns the festivities, where everyone competes to see the most beautiful flowers
